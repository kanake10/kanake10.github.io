Hey, I'm Ezra, a Mobile Engineer and an indie developer who loves working on open source projects and contributing to them outside of work.

## What I do

I specialize in building Android apps with **Jetpack Compose**, **Clean 
Architecture** and **Kotlin**. I care a lot about writing code that scales, 
is testable and easy for other engineers to work with.

I also maintain the [Translate Android SDK](https://github.com/kanake10/translate-android) on 
[Maven Central](https://central.sonatype.com/artifact/io.github.kanake10/translate) — a modular translation SDK with optional voice (TTS/STT) and 
camera (ML Kit OCR) plugins. I do write about Android patterns, SDKs and Kotlin on 
[Medium](https://medium.com/@10kanake).
