About

Hey, I’m Ezra — an Android Engineer based in Nairobi, Kenya.

I currently work at Earth Hero, building climate-action tools that help people make a real environmental impact. Before that I’ve contributed to organizations including World Agroforestry, GreenStand, and Machini.

What I do

I specialize in building Android apps with Jetpack Compose, Clean Architecture, and Kotlin. I care a lot about writing code that scales — modular, testable, and easy for other engineers to work with.

I also maintain the TranslatePlus Android SDK (io.github.kanake10) on Maven Central — a modular translation SDK with optional voice (TTS/STT) and camera (ML Kit OCR) plugins.

Writing

I write about Android patterns, SDKs, and Kotlin on Medium and have contributed technical articles to Baeldung and LogRocket.

Stack

Kotlin · Jetpack Compose · MVVM · Clean Architecture · Kotlin Multiplatform · Ktor · Hilt · Coroutines · ML Kit · Retrofit
